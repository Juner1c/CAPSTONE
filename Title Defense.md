
### **TITLE**

STATUSIFIER : A Web-Based Recruitment Management System with Automated Applicant Status Tracking and Communication for HR Managers

**Brief explanation of the title:**

- **STATUSIFIER** is the name of the proposed system, emphasizing applicant status updates and tracking.
- **Web-Based** means the system is accessible through a web browser.
- **Recruitment Management System** refers to software that assists HR managers in organizing and managing recruitment activities.
- **Automated Applicant Status Tracking and Communication** means the system automatically tracks applicants through the recruitment process and sends status notifications to keep them informed.
- **For HR Managers** identifies the primary users of the system.

### **INTRODUCTION**

Recruitment is a crucial function in every organization because it ensures that qualified individuals are selected to fill available positions. As companies receive increasing numbers of applications, managing recruitment manually through spreadsheets, emails, and paper records can become time-consuming and prone to errors. These methods often result in disorganized applicant records, delayed communication, and difficulty in monitoring the progress of applicants throughout the hiring process.

One common concern in recruitment is the lack of timely communication between employers and applicants. Many applicants are left waiting without updates regarding their application status, while HR managers spend significant time manually responding to inquiries and updating records. These challenges affect both recruitment efficiency and the applicant experience.

To address these issues, the researchers propose **STATUSIFIER**, a web-based recruitment management system designed to centralize recruitment-related information, automate applicant status tracking, and streamline communication between HR managers and applicants. The proposed system aims to improve recruitment efficiency by providing a single platform where HR personnel can manage applicants, monitor recruitment progress, and send automated email notifications whenever an applicant's status changes.

### **Contents (System Overview)**

The proposed system will provide the following functionalities:

- Secure login for HR managers and applicants.
- Job posting and vacancy management.
- Online applicant registration and resume submission.
- Centralized applicant management.
- Automated applicant status tracking.
- Email notifications for application status updates.
- Recruitment dashboard for HR managers.
- Import and export of applicant records using Microsoft Excel.
- Generation of recruitment reports.

The system will use **PostgreSQL** as its primary database for storing recruitment data, while **Microsoft Excel** will be supported for importing, exporting, and maintaining backup copies of applicant records.

### **OBJECTIVE**S

#### General Objective

To develop **STATUSIFIER**, a web-based recruitment management system that improves applicant tracking, communication, and recruitment management through process automation.

#### Specific Objectives

1. To provide a centralized platform for managing applicant information and recruitment records.
2. To automate applicant status tracking throughout the recruitment process.
3. To provide timely email notifications regarding application status changes.
4. To assist HR managers in monitoring applicants efficiently using a centralized dashboard.
5. To support Excel import and export for applicant data migration, reporting, and backup purposes.
6. To improve the overall efficiency and organization of recruitment activities.

### **STATEMENT OF THE PROBLEM**

#### General Problem

How can a web-based recruitment management system improve applicant tracking and communication between HR managers and job applicants?

#### Specific Problems

1. How can applicant information be managed in a centralized recruitment system?
2. How can applicant status updates be automated to reduce manual administrative work?
3. How can applicants receive timely and consistent updates regarding their application status?
4. How can HR managers efficiently monitor applicants throughout the recruitment process?
5. How can recruitment records be securely maintained while supporting Excel-based reporting and backup?

### **CONCLUSION**

The proposed study introduces **STATUSIFIER**, a web-based recruitment management system that addresses common challenges in recruitment by centralizing applicant information, automating status tracking, and improving communication between HR managers and applicants. By integrating applicant management, email notifications, and Excel import/export capabilities into a single platform, the system aims to reduce manual administrative work, improve recruitment efficiency, and enhance the overall hiring experience.

The researchers believe that STATUSIFIER offers a practical and feasible solution for organizations seeking to modernize their recruitment process while maintaining compatibility with existing tools such as Microsoft Excel. If approved, the project has the potential to contribute to more organized, transparent, and efficient recruitment management.
